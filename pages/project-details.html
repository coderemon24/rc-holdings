Client

About the Project

The Commercial Tower is a state-of-the-art 25-story mixed-use development in the heart of Dhaka's business district. Kaizen Holdings was responsible for the complete design and construction of this landmark project, which combines office spaces, retail areas, and luxury apartments.

Our team faced significant challenges with the site's limited footprint and high water table, which we overcame through innovative foundation design and construction techniques. The project was completed on time and within budget, receiving LEED Gold certification for its sustainable features.

Key Features

  • LEED Gold Certified
  • Energy-efficient facade
  • Smart building technology
  • Underground parking for 200 vehicles
  • Rooftop garden and terrace
  • Earthquake-resistant structure
